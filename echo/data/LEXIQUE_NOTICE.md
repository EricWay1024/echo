# Vendored data: Lexique 3.83

`lexique_fr.tsv.gz` is a slimmed subset (columns: `ortho`, `phon`, `freqfilms2`)
of **Lexique 3.83** from <http://www.lexique.org>, used here for offline
French word-level IPA (via the `phon` → IPA mapping in `echo/phon.py`).

Lexique is distributed openly for research/educational use. Please cite:

> New, B., Pallier, C., Brysbaert, M., & Ferrand, L. (2004). Lexique 2: A new
> French lexical database. *Behavior Research Methods, Instruments, & Computers.*

Full dataset and license: http://www.lexique.org
